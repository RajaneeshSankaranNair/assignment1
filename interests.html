Interest in Information Technology

I didn’t have much knowledge about computers or Information Technology until my uncle told me to study Diploma in Mechanical Engineering with CNC machine language. I was so amazed about fully artificial intelligence equipped metal cutting machines that is CNC machines. I was so curious with artificial intelligence since then. My teachers had suggested to learn more about IT but I knew that I could not afford to do so because IT was one of the most expensive course in India during that time, so decided to join a collage near my home to study a short time machine language programming course is called CNC programming.

I got a job in Suzuki Motors, India through college’s career placement program. I was truly amazed to see artificial intelligence enabled CNC machines and robots in car assembly areas, welding shop, paint shop and logistic-warehouse areas. I have got variety of mechanical related IT trainings which includes sketch the 2D and 3D drawings with the help of designing and drafting computer softwares AutoCAD and Master Cam.

I am a 39-year-old man now but I have an enthusiastic and energic heart that always pushing me to achieve the goal that is study IT. When it comes to choosing a University, I have to consider plenty of factors because I am not a teenager, I don’t have much time left in my life, I can afford to waste three or four years of time because of a wrong decision. I had a clear picture about what should I expect from a university like the superior quality of education, experience in teaching industry, reputation and ranking, feedbacks from students, study options and student support. I found RMIT after a long and deep research and finalized straight away as I realized that with more than 100 years of heritage and experience, RMIT provide much more than what I expect from a University.
